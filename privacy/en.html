Hello! Welcome to Plyxo Games. This fun collection of games is developed by Sandeep Biswal G. We want to be super clear about how we handle your information. Your privacy is a big deal to us, and this policy is here to explain everything in simple terms.

Plyxo Games is designed for entertainment, learning, and friendly competition. By using our games, you agree to what's written in this policy.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. The app does not use analytics tools, crash reporting services, or tracking SDKs. We do not collect names, email addresses, photos, location data, or any other personal details. Users may contact us voluntarily by email for support, and any information shared this way is used only to respond to the request. Third-party advertising services used in the app may collect limited technical information as described in their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services (Google AdMob)

To keep Plyxo Games free for everyone, we use a service from Google called AdMob to show ads. AdMob is a third-party service, which means it's not run by us, but by Google. They have their own way of doing things and their own privacy policy.

This service may collect some data to show ads, which is collected and processed by Google, not by us. This could include your device ID, location (in a general sense, like your country), and how you use the app. We recommend you check out Google's Privacy Policy to understand exactly what they do.

Ads

Just to be clear, the ads you see in the game are provided by Google AdMob. We don't control the specific ads you see. These ads help us pay the bills and continue developing more cool games for you to enjoy. We try our best to ensure the ads are appropriate for everyone.

Log Data

We do not collect or store any log data, crash reports, or diagnostic information directly. The app does not use analytics tools or crash reporting services. If an error occurs, we do not automatically receive any technical data from the app.

Cookies

Cookies are small files with a bit of data, often used as anonymous unique identifiers. Our app itself doesn't use these "cookies" directly. However, the third-party services we use, like Google AdMob, might use them to collect information and improve their services. Any such data is handled according to the privacy policies of the third-party services and your device settings.

Children’s Privacy

Our games are for everyone to enjoy. However, we do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided personal information through third-party services used by the app, please contact us. Since the app does not collect or store personal information, we do not retain such data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Sometimes, we might need to update this privacy policy. We may do this to reflect changes in our services or for other legal reasons. We will post the new privacy policy on this page. We suggest you check this page from time to time to stay updated. Any changes are effective as soon as they are posted here.

Contact Us

Got any questions? Or any suggestions? We'd love to hear from you! If you have any concerns about this Privacy Policy or anything else, please don't hesitate to get in touch.
